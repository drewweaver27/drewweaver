GRG Marketplace

ECommerce WordPress Site

Q4 2018 - Q1 2019

GRGMarketplace was my first client project, and I acted as the sole designer and developer. The site is a multi-vendor Ecommerce site built with WordPress and WooCommerce. Designed for a group of music producers, the site allows for each producer to sell their music content on their own sales "profiles" to other musicians. The site has a reported gross income of $125,000+ since it's Q1 2019 launch. Besides the monetary value produced by the site, the site has achieved it's main goal of helping producers advance their music careers while helping others to create great music.
